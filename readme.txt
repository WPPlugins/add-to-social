Add To Social - Share your posts

== Description ==

Adds a list of XHTML compliant graphic links at the end of your posts that allow your visitors to easily submit them to a number of social bookmarking sites.

Social sites:

    * Svejo
    * Favit
    * Facebook
    * Twitter
    * Edno23
    * Dao
	* Delicious
	* Google Buzz
    * Google Bookmarks
    * Digg


	
[Demo](http://dev.svil4ok.info/wordpress/add-to-social/) | [Plugin information](http://dev.svil4ok.info/wordpress/add-to-social/)

== Installation ==


1. Unpack the download-package.

2. Upload 'add-to-social' folder to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory

3. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

5. Done and Ready.
